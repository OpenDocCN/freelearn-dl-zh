About the Contributor

Anthony Brown

Former Head of Data and AI, Procurement Sciences

Current Senior Strategic Account Director at Salesforce

Anthony Brown transforms how aerospace and defense contractors win and execute government business through the strategic application of AI, data science, and advanced analytics. With 15+ years of building mission-critical solutions for the public sector, Anthony has consistently delivered innovations that drive measurable outcomes.

Earlier in his career, Anthony served as Director of Data and AI at General Dynamics Information Technology, where he led enterprise-wide AI governance across Federal Civilian, Defense, Homeland Security, Health, and State sectors. By piloting five transformative generative AI use cases and chairing the company’s AI Steering Committee, he established the strategic framework that continues to shape how the organization leverages emerging technologies.

Anthony’s unique value comes from his combination of technical depth and practical government experience. At Tableau/Salesforce, he developed key relationships with federal system integrators like Lockheed Martin, SAIC, and Northrop Grumman, securing multi-million-dollar contracts through his consultative approach to solving agency challenges. This insider understanding of both the technology and procurement landscape allows him to bridge gaps that others can’t see.
