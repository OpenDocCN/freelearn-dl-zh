Part 1

Planning Your Presentation Content and Design

In this first part of the book, you’ll develop a solid understanding of how to plan your presentation content and design. We’ll be discussing questions that will help you plan your content before you open your PowerPoint application, and industry best practices to design better visuals. We’ll also learn how to leverage the various masters in PowerPoint. By the end of this part, you’ll have the knowledge needed to start planning your content and create or adapt your presentation template.

This part includes the following chapters:

  • Chapter 1 , Analyzing Your Audience and Presentation Delivery Needs
  • Chapter 2 , Using Industry Best Practices to Design Better Visuals
  • Chapter 3 , Leveraging PowerPoint’s Slide Master for Design
  • Chapter 4 , Using PowerPoint’s Document Masters for Handouts and Notes
