Part 2

Creating More Creative and Impactful Visuals

In the second part of the book, we’ll discuss how to leverage AI tools, such as M365 Copilot, as a content creation starting point. We’ll also learn about the visual and multimedia tools in the application that can help avoid text-heavy slides while adding visual interest with meaningful animations, transitions, and interactivity in presentation content. A chapter on third-party add-ins will put forward some of the best productivity and creative tools in the industry. By the end of this part, you’ll have the knowledge needed to create more creative and impactful visuals in your presentations.

This part includes the following chapters:

  • Chapter 5 , Starting Your Presentation with M365 Copilot
  • Chapter 6 , Using Artificial Intelligence to Improve Your Visuals
  • Chapter 7 , Adding and Modifying Visual Elements
  • Chapter 8 , Adding and Modifying Multimedia Elements
  • Chapter 9 , Working with Transitions and Animations
  • Chapter 10 , Building Flexibility and Interactivity into Your Presentations
  • Chapter 11 , Using PowerPoint Third-Party Add-Ins
