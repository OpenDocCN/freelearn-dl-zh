Part 3

Improving Presentation Delivery

In the third part of the book, we’ll discuss how to leverage some great PowerPoint tools to practice presentation delivery, with practice being an essential element of having a memorable effect on audiences. We’ll also discuss some useful tools and applications in Teams that will help engage virtual audiences. By the end of this part of the book, you’ll have the knowledge needed to deliver presentations in a more impactful and engaging way to make presentations more memorable.

This part includes the following chapters:

  • Chapter 12 , Practicing Your Presentation Delivery
  • Chapter 13 , Using Presenter View
  • Chapter 14 , Using PowerPoint Live in Microsoft Teams
