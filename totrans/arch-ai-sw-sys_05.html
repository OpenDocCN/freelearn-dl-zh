Part 2

Architecting AI Systems

The second part of the book walks you through more detailed explanations, guides, and lessons learned to build AI-enabled systems. This part of the book starts with conceptual design, one of the most critical phases led by an architect. The final chapters of this section go into more detail on the architect’s role in going from conceptual design to system testing. Finally, a case study is highlighted as a real and common challenge where an AI-enabled system can readily transform a business problem.

The following chapters are included in this section:

  • Chapter 4, Conceptual Design for AI Systems
  • Chapter 5, Requirements and Architecture for AI Pipelines
  • Chapter 6, Design, Integration, and Testing
  • Chapter 7, Architecting a Generative AI System – A Case Study
