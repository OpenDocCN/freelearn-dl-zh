About the Contributor

Kyrylo (Kip) Pogrebenko

CEO and Co-Founder at Medloaner

With a background spanning healthcare marketing, data science, and executive leadership, Kip has honed his expertise in creating and scaling impactful technology solutions. At Medloaner, he focuses on improving surgical instrument supply chains with AI and computer vision technologies. His prior roles include:

  • Vice President of Product at Doceree, where he led the development of innovative healthcare data platforms.
  • Senior Ad Tech Consultant at Amazon, where he integrated advanced machine learning solutions into e-commerce analytics.

These experiences have equipped him with a deep understanding of data-driven strategies, product commercialization, and regulatory challenges in the healthcare sector.

Kip envisions a future where data not only improves patient outcomes but also transforms global healthcare systems. He is particularly interested in the potential of blockchain technology to anonymize and secure healthcare data so it can be used for advanced analytics. Kip’s ultimate goal is to harness technology to drive predictive insights, improve resource allocation, and create a more efficient and equitable healthcare ecosystem.
