Part 2: Implementing Dynamics 365 AI Across Business Functions

This part is all about exploring the ways Dynamics 365 AI can make current processes better and open up new possibilities for businesses to innovate and connect with their customers and markets.

  • Chapter 4, Driving Customer Service Excellence with Dynamics 365 AI
